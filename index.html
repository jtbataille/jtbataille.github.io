About me

Welcome to my site! My name is Jarrod Bataille, and I am a full-stack developer. I was drawn into the passion of web development while working as an English teacher in China, Italy, and later in the United States. After seeing the disparities among groups of students and groups of adults, the need I saw there kindled in me the fire of building programs and applications aimed at aiding in web literacy and access--something I consider to be critical for web development--in the form of various applications and websites. With this passion for programming in mind, you as the user will find some of the many projects I have completed regarding this goal. Please browse through the site, and send in any inquiries using the contact form found on my site!
